Elite Athlete's Playbook

Leave the Ego Behind

A 20kg hold means nothing if your form is shaky. One clean, stable rep outweighs ten ego-driven sets. Control is the ultimate flex.

Straight Arm Protocol

Lock your elbows and engage your scapula like they're forged from steel. Power leakage happens when you lose tension—stay rigid.

Tendon Integrity

Muscles recover fast, but tendons take years. Respect the load on your joints. If you feel sharp pain, stop. Longevity beats speed.

The Mental Lock

Calisthenics is 90% nervous system. Visualize the hold, feel the tension in every fiber, and own the weight before you lift it.
